How Buddy4Study

Works

Register and create your profile

  • Sign up with Google/Mobile number or email id
  • Verify your mobile number and email address
  • Complete your profile i.e., present class, income,
    location, gender and other required fields
  • Download Buddy4Study app to keep track of scholarships

Get notified for matching scholarships

  • Based on your profile you will receive matching
    scholarship email/sms/whatsapp/app notifications
  • Keep your profile updated to receive relevant notifications, you can unsubscribe if you don't want to receive notifications anymore

Apply for scholarships

  • Apply for scholarships with easy to fill form - most of your information will be prefilled
  • Upload high-quality documents with all fields visible
  • Regularly monitor your email/mobile for Buddy4Study notifications
  • Respond to questions in telephonic interviews

Become a scholar

  • If you are selected as a scholar, you will receive notifications from Buddy4Study
  • You can also check results on scholarship results page
  • Upload your attested documents on Buddy4Study and provide bank information
  • Receive scholarship award directly in your bank account
